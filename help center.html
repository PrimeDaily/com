Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password? ‚ñº

To reset your password, click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Enter your email address and we'll send you a secure link to create a new password. The link will expire in 24 hours for security purposes.

How can I update my profile information? ‚ñº

Go to your account settings by clicking on your profile picture in the top right corner, then select "Account Settings." You can update your name, email, phone number, and notification preferences from there.

How do I delete my account? ‚ñº

Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. To delete your account, go to Account Settings > Privacy & Security > Delete Account. You'll need to confirm your password and provide feedback about why you're leaving.

What subscription plans do you offer? ‚ñº

We offer three plans: Basic (free with limited articles), Premium ($9.99/month with unlimited access), and Premium+ ($19.99/month with premium content and ad-free experience). All paid plans include mobile app access.

How do I cancel my subscription? ‚ñº

You can cancel your subscription anytime from your Account Settings > Subscription. Your access will continue until the end of your current billing period. No cancellation fees apply, and you can resubscribe at any time.

Why was my payment declined? ‚ñº

Payment declines can happen due to insufficient funds, expired cards, or security measures. Check your card details, ensure sufficient balance, and contact your bank if the issue persists. You can update your payment method in Account Settings.

Why won't the website load properly? ‚ñº

Try clearing your browser cache and cookies, disable ad blockers temporarily, or try a different browser. If the issue persists, check your internet connection or try accessing the site from an incognito/private window.

Is there a mobile app available? ‚ñº

Yes! Our mobile app is available for both iOS and Android. Premium subscribers get the full app experience with offline reading, push notifications, and exclusive mobile-only features. Download from the App Store or Google Play.

How do I report a technical issue? ‚ñº

Use the "Report Issue" button at the bottom of any page, or email our technical support team at tech@primedailynews.com. Include details about your device, browser, and the specific problem you're experiencing for faster resolution.
